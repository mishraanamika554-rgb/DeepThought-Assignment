# DeepThought AI Systems Analyst Assignment

## Overview

This repository contains my submission for the DeepThought AI Systems Analyst Assignment. The objective of this assignment was to identify and evaluate potential Federer-fit companies within Hyderabad across Specialty Manufacturing and Adjacent Specialty segments.

The assignment involved researching companies, evaluating them using the provided E1, E2, and C3-C8 criteria, and creating a structured company database supported by a documented methodology. In addition, a scalable sourcing strategy was developed to demonstrate how a database of 1000 qualified companies could be built within a defined timeline.

## Contents

This repository includes:

* Company Research Spreadsheet (25 shortlisted companies)
* Methodology Document
* 1000 Company Sourcing Proposal
* Hand-Drawn Process Diagram

## Research Approach

The research process involved identifying Hyderabad-based companies operating in selected target segments and evaluating them against the assignment framework. Publicly available sources such as company websites, business directories, LinkedIn, and industry information were used for data collection and validation.

The companies were assessed using the provided criteria:

* E1: Producer Qualification
* E2: Accessibility Qualification
* C3: Differentiation
* C4: Decision-Maker Quality
* C5: Growing Sector
* C6: Growth Signals
* C7: Systems Maturity
* C8: Leadership Succession

## 1000 Company Sourcing Strategy

A structured sourcing model was designed to scale company discovery through multiple channels including LinkedIn, company websites, industry directories, and public business databases. The process includes qualification filters, scoring mechanisms, manual verification, and quality control measures to ensure high-confidence company identification.

## AI Usage

AI tools were used to assist with information organization, data structuring, and workflow planning. All final outputs were reviewed and organized before inclusion in the submission.
